Services We Offer

At SME Bank Parcel Centre, we are dedicated to providing top-notch parcel management services to ensure your parcels are handled efficiently and effectively. Our range of services includes:

  • Parcel Reception and Storage: We receive parcels on behalf of our clients and securely store them until they are ready for collection.
  • Tracking and Notification: We provide tracking numbers for all parcels and notify clients upon arrival.
  • Easy Pickup: Our convenient location and efficient process make parcel pickup quick and hassle-free.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated support team is available to assist with any queries or concerns you may have.
  • Secure Handling: We ensure that all parcels are handled with care and stored in a secure environment.

For more information about our services or to track your parcel, please visit our Home page.
